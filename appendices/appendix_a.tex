\section{Survey Instrument}
\label{app:survey}

This appendix presents the survey instrument used in the study.

\subsection{Section A: Demographics}
\begin{enumerate}
    \item What is your age group?
    \begin{enumerate}[label=\alph*)]
        \item 18–34
        \item 35–50
        \item 51+
    \end{enumerate}

    \item What is your gender?
    \begin{enumerate}[label=\alph*)]
        \item Male
        \item Female
        \item Other / Prefer not to say
    \end{enumerate}
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Section B: Constructs}
\noindent\textit{Please indicate your level of agreement with each statement
(1 = Strongly Disagree, 7 = Strongly Agree).}

\begin{enumerate}
    \item Statement representing item 1 of Construct A.
    \item Statement representing item 2 of Construct A.
    \item Statement representing item 3 of Construct A.
\end{enumerate}
