\section{Methods}

This chapter describes the research design, participants, data collection procedures, measures, and analytical strategy employed in your study. Provide enough detail so that other researchers could replicate your study.

Describe the overall research design (e.g., quantitative, qualitative, or mixed-methods). For example, mixed-methods studies frequently employ a sequential explanatory design \parencite{Creswell2018}.
